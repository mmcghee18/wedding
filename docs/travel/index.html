Travel

Getting to Waterfall Lodge

Waterfall Lodge has limited parking and does not allow people to park onsite unless they're staying there overnight. If you're staying elsewhere on Saturday night, we have a shuttle that will pick you up ~5 minutes out from Waterfall Lodge and bring you in. The shuttle pick-up is at this address: St Peter & St Paul Orthodox. You should park in the green zone on the map below. Plan to catch a shuttle between 2:15 - 2:45pm.

The shuttles will also bring guests back to their cars at the end of the reception. The earliest shuttle will leave at 8:30pm. The latest shuttles will run until 1am bringing guests to and from the afterparty.

If you're staying in a cabin on Saturday night after the ceremony, can just drive into and park at Waterfall Lodge. You can arrive as early as 1pm.

Airports

For out of town guests, we recommend flying into San Francisco (SFO) or San Jose (SJC) airports.

From there, you can drive down to Ben Lomond and Santa Cruz - it is about an hour and a half from SFO and 45 minutes from SJC.

Reach out at michellemegan.2024@gmail.com if you need help coordinating a ride from the airport.

Where to stay

  • Waterfall Lodge (Saturday night only) - Update: the cabins are filled! If you're staying in a cabin you should have received an email with instructions on how to reserve it.
  • Hotels - There are a number of nearby hotels.
    • Quality Inn & Suites Santa Cruz Mountains (Google Maps)
      Just 5 minutes from Waterfall Lodge.
    • Historic Brookdale Lodge (Google Maps)
      Also close to Waterfall Lodge.
    • Hampton Inn Santa Cruz West (Google Maps)
      Santa Cruz is also a great place to stay and explore! This hotel is right across the street from where we'll be having welcome drinks on Friday, and is a 30 minute drive to Waterfall Lodge.
  • Airbnb / VRBO - There are a number of options, in Ben Lomond, Santa Cruz, or other surrounding areas.
