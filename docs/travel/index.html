Travel

Getting there

For out of town guests, we recommend flying into San Francisco (SFO) or San Jose (SJC) airports.

From there, you can drive down to Ben Lomond and Santa Cruz - it is about an hour and a half from SFO and 45 minutes from SJC.

Reach out at michellemegan.2024@gmail.com if you need help coordinating a ride from the airport. We'll do our best to connect people who could potentially carpool!

Important note about driving to the Saturday ceremony/reception:

Waterfall Lodge has limited parking and does not allow people to park onsite unless they're staying there overnight. If you're staying elsewhere on Saturday night, we have a shuttle that will pick you up ~5 minutes out from Waterfall Lodge and bring you in. The shuttle pick-up is at this address: St Peter & St Paul Orthodox. Plan to catch a shuttle around 2:30pm, stay tuned for more details.

The shuttles will also bring guests back to their cars at the end of the reception.

If you're staying in a cabin on Saturday night after the ceremony, you are able to drive into and park at Waterfall Lodge.

Where to stay

  • Waterfall Lodge (Saturday night only) - Update: the cabins are filled! If you're staying in a cabin you should have received an email with instructions on how to reserve it.
  • Hotels - There are a number of nearby hotels.
    • Quality Inn & Suites Santa Cruz Mountains (Google Maps)
      Just 5 minutes from Waterfall Lodge.
    • Historic Brookdale Lodge (Google Maps)
      Also close to Waterfall Lodge.
    • Hampton Inn Santa Cruz West (Google Maps)
      Santa Cruz is also a great place to stay and explore! This hotel is right across the street from where we'll be having welcome drinks on Friday, and is a 30 minute drive to Waterfall Lodge.
  • Airbnb / VRBO - There are a number of options, in Ben Lomond, Santa Cruz, or other surrounding areas.
